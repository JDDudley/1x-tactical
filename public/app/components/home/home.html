1X Mission

Our Mission at 1X Tactical is three fold:

  1. Provide elite training to our team members that will help them to keep themselves, family and community safe during different types of threats whether it is a natural disaster or criminal attack.
  2. Provide our Team Members access to different tools of survival at close to wholesale pricing in our Armory.
  3. Support our Veterans by employing those who have honorably served in the Armed Forces and who are Subject Matter Experts in different fields of survival as instructors.
